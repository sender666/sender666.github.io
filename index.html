Biography

I am currently a final year Ph.D. candidate at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, under the esteemed supervision of Prof. Jian Wu. I am scheduled to graduate in July, 2025. I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science from Hunan University in July 2020, where I was advised by Prof. Xin Liao at the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering. I received my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Hunan University in July 2017, also from the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering.

Research Interests

Computer Vision, AI for Science. I focus on Computer Vision methods and their applications for Sciences, including knowledge distillation and fusion for real-world classification and segmentation tasks.
