Biography

I am currently a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, under the esteemed supervision of Prof. Jian Wu. I am scheduled to graduate in July, 2025. I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science from Hunan University in July 2020, where I was advised by Prof. Xin Liao at the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering. I received my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Hunan University in July 2017, also from the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering. Prior to my academic journey at Hunan University, I spent my early years in my hometown, Huining, Gansu province.

Research Interests

AI for Science, Computer Vision, and Multimedia Security. I focus on Computer Vision methods and their applications for Sciences, including knowledge distillation and fusion for image classification and segmentation.
