About

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Jian Wu. I am scheduled to graduate in Sep 2025.

I obtained my M.S. in Computer Science from Hunan University (July 2020), advised by Prof. Xin Liao at the College of Computer Science and Electronic Engineering. I received my B.S. in Software Engineering from Hunan University in July 2017.

My research focuses on Computer Vision and AI for Science, including knowledge distillation and fusion for real-world classification and segmentation tasks.
